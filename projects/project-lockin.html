Lockin.tech

Young Enterprise Project - Educational Focus App

Overview

Lockin.tech is an educational app designed to help students stay focused and productive during their study sessions. Built as part of a Young Enterprise project, this app provides a streamlined approach to focused studying.

The Problem

Students struggle to maintain focus during study sessions, often getting distracted by their devices. Existing solutions were either too complicated or didn't address the core issue of building sustainable study habits.

The Solution

Lockin.tech provides a streamlined approach to focused studying, combining proven productivity techniques with a clean, distraction-free interface. The app helps students structure their study sessions and track their progress over time.

Key Features

  • Focus timer with customizable session lengths
  • Progress tracking and statistics
  • Clean, minimalist interface
  • Subscription-based premium features

What I Learned

Building Lockin.tech was an incredible learning experience that taught me far more than just coding:

  • Full Development Lifecycle: From initial concept and design through to deployment on app stores
  • Payment Integration: Implementing subscription-based monetization using RevenueCat and Stripe
  • UI/UX Design: Creating intuitive interfaces that users actually want to use
  • App Store Process: Navigating submission requirements, reviews, and approvals
  • Legal Knowledge: Understanding trademark law, privacy policies, and terms of service
  • Business Skills: The commercial side of app development and monetization strategies
