About Me

Aspiring doctor with a passion for science and technology

Who I Am

I'm Karl Galliano — a Year 12 student at Bayside Comprehensive School in Gibraltar, aspiring to become a doctor with a deep fascination for immunology and neuroscience.

My academic journey centres around the sciences. I'm currently studying A-Levels in Biology, Chemistry, Mathematics, and Further Mathematics. Understanding how the human body defends itself through the immune system and how the brain processes information are questions that drive my scientific curiosity.

Alongside my academic pursuits, I've developed a passion for app development. Learning Flutter and Dart has allowed me to turn ideas into functional applications, culminating in the creation of Lockin.tech — an educational app designed to help students stay focused and productive.

Looking ahead, I aspire to study Medicine at a UK university, where I can pursue my passion for understanding the human body and helping others through a career in healthcare.
