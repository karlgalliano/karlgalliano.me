Research Interests

Areas I'm passionate about exploring

Immunology

The immune system's ability to distinguish self from non-self, recognise and eliminate pathogens, and remember previous infections is one of nature's most remarkable achievements.

Specific Interests:

  • Adaptive immune responses and antibody production
  • T-cell mediated immunity
  • Immunological memory and vaccination
  • Autoimmune disorders and immune regulation

Neuroscience

Understanding how the brain processes information, forms memories, and generates consciousness represents one of science's greatest challenges.

Specific Interests:

  • Neural signalling and synaptic transmission
  • Memory formation and learning
  • Brain plasticity and development
  • The molecular basis of neurological disorders

Mathematics in Medicine

Mathematics provides powerful tools for understanding biological systems, from modelling disease spread to analysing complex physiological data.

Specific Interests:

  • Statistical analysis in clinical research
  • Mathematical modelling of biological systems
  • Epidemiological models and disease prediction
  • Data-driven approaches to medical diagnosis

A-Level Studies

Key Topics
